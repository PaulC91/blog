About Me

Hello! I’m Paul and this is a personal blog to document my forays into all things R, data & visualisation.

Originally from Edinburgh, Scotland, I graduated with an MA in Applied Economics from the University of St Andrews in 2013.

I’m now working for data consultancy Culture of Insight in London as an analyst, R programmer and shiny developer.

Feel free to get in touch via any of the channels below. You can check out some of my work on the blog page, projects page, or over on the Culture of Insight blog.

We’re always looking to get involved in new data projects at Culture of Insight, so if you’re interested in working together, shoot us a note here.

Cheers!
